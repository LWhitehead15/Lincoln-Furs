Meet rules:

  • • All LincsFurs meets are strictly 18+.
  • • Bigotry of any kind will not be tolerated.
    • o This includes, but is not limited to: racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, and misogyny.
  • • Respect all attendees and staff.
  • • Do not touch people or handle their property (fursuits included) without consent.
  • • Some of our venues do not permit outside food and beverages. Please respect this and any other venue policies.
  • • Costumes must be SFW, and must not include kink gear.
    • o Animal collars are allowed, but leashes are not.
    • o If you’re unsure if something counts as kink gear, please contact our admin team with a photo of the item, and we’ll be happy to advise.
  • • Costumes must not include realistic weaponry or tactical gear.
    • o If you’re unsure if something counts as a realistic weapon, please contact our admin team with a photo of the item, and we’ll be happy to advise. For the safety of attendees, we will err on the side of caution.
If you feel unsafe or uncomfortable at any time, please make a staff member aware either in person or via Telegram as soon as possible.

Chat rules:

  • • All LincsFurs online spaces are strictly 18+.
  • • Bigotry of any kind will not be tolerated.
    • o This includes, but is not limited to: racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, and misogyny.
  • • Keep things civil and pleasant.
  • • Avoid sticker / image spam.
  • • LincsFurs is a Safe for Work (SFW) space, this includes profile pictures, stickers, images and hyperlinks. General discussion around “adult” topics is permitted in moderation and at the discretion of the admin team.
  • • We reserve the right to prohibit particular topics / stickers which are likely to distress other attendees. Although we make no judgements on the interests / kinks of an individual we will always err on the side of ensuring the comfort of our attendees.
  • • Do not monopolize our online chats, if you are having a long conversation between a small group please consider taking it to DMs.
  • • Advertising requires permission from an admin before posting.
    • o If you wish to post a link or advert to commissions / streams / services, please contact our admin team for permission.
  • • Please respect content creators in our online spaces, this includes removing watermarks from images, sharing AI generated content, etc.
If there are any problems or suggestions regarding our online spaces, please make a staff member on there aware as soon as possible.
