Meet rules:

  • • All official Lincoln Furs meets are strictly 18+.
  • • Acts of hate speech and discrimination will not be tolerated.
  • • Antisocial behaviour will not be accepted. You may be warned or asked to leave the venue if causing harm or disruption to other attendees, venue staff or the general public.
  • • Respect fellow attendees and fursuiters, do not harass or touch people or handle their property (fursuits included) without consent.
  • • Likewise, please respect people’s boundaries and do not assume all people are comfortable with nsfw content or topics.
  • • Ask permission before taking / sharing photos of individuals and fursuiters where possible.
  • • Some of our venues do not permit outside food and beverages, please respect this and any other venue policies.
  • • The possession, use or sale of illicit substances is prohibited.
  • • Practice self care - be sure to look after yourself, stay hydrated and make staff aware of any problems.
If you feel unsafe or uncomfortable at any time, please make a staff member aware either in person or via Telegram as soon as possible.

Chat rules:

  • • All Lincoln Furs online spaces are strictly 18+.
  • • No hate speech, be respectful of others.
  • • No illegal content will be permitted.
  • • Keep things civil and pleasant.
  • • Avoid sticker / image spam.
  • • Keep all posts ‘Safe for Work’.
  • • Links to / previews of NSFW content may be allowed depending on the specific rules of that online space.
  • • Do not monopolize our online chats, if you are having a long conversation between a small group please consider taking it to DMs.
  • • Self-Promotion is permitted but must closely follow the rules associated with that online space.
  • • Please respect content creators in our online spaces, if they ask to be credited for event photography, artwork etc, please do so.
If there are any problems or suggestions regarding our online spaces, please make a staff member on there aware as soon as possible.
