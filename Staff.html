Our Staff Team

Here are the community members who organise these meets and serve as your point of contact for support:

Mango Birb

He/Him

Mango joined Lincoln Furs in 2021 and became part of the staff team as the group's resident IT guy. He designed this website in 2023 and continues to maintain it behind the scenes. While you may not see him at every meet, he's usually lurking online and is always happy to chat.

Outside the community, Mango works in IT consultancy as a developer and tester. His interests include visual novel writing & coding, film photography, and exploring the world of perfumery.

Bramble

They/It

Bramble can be spotted running around meets either as Moss, a mushroom themed Skulldog or Rust, a Mad Max inspired striped Hyena.

They’ve been part of LincsFurs since 2022 and help out at meets by keeping an eye on things, checking that people are comfortable, and making sure newcomers feel welcome. If you’re new, overwhelmed, or just fancy a chat, Bramble is always happy to help.

Pequod

He/Him

Pequod joined at the tail end of 2019 and was part of the last LincsFurs expedition up Steep Hill. Since then, at nearly every meet he has lent a hand whether it be handling the suit walk or taking photos of the meet.

Eventually he was brought onto the team as a volunteer in 2023, invited to staff in 2024, and finally took over as chair and our "Dear Leader" in 2025.

In his spare time he enjoys gaming, or going around Lincoln for walks and playing Pokémon Go.

Materies

He/Him

Materies is a trained swordswolf with medals under his belt from longsword tournements, as well as being a ex-instructor of survival skills in scouting. Alongside these, he streams on Twitch and produces story videos on YouTube as well as supporting other creators.

Materies joined the furry community in the summer of 2021, quickly become staff at a larger furmeet. He later left that meet for more local meets and joined the Lincsfurs staff team in early 2026. You can often find him at meets, on the fursuit walk and in the Discord.

Xantin

He/Him

Xantin has been part of the Fandom for over 20 years, joining the NorthernFurs group in around 2004.

He joined LincsFurs in 2022 and was asked to join the staff team in 2024 as a Telegram admin.

In the real world he’s a reporting & SQL development manager with a love for baking, Lego, board gaming & wasting money on things he doesn’t need.

In the fandom outside of LincsFurs he's the Community Lead for LeedsFurs and one of the founder members of the Yorkshire Furs Summer Party.

Ozzysaurus

He/Him

0zzysaurus joined the Furry fandom in 2015 and began attending LincsFurs as his primary local meet at the beginning of 2023.

He was added to the staff team in 2025, assisting with social media management, chat moderation, and in-person volunteering.

Outside of meets, 0zzy is a digital artist, playwright, and an aspiring academic. He enjoys watching American football, going fishing, and playing video games.

Term

She/Her

After a four year stint away, Term returned to Lincoln in February 2025 and has been causing trouble ever since.

She loves chickens, arts and crafts, writing, interesting rocks and cool bugs.

When not running around as a giant deer with wings at meets, Term loves spending time sketching other peoples' OCs, so feel free to ask for a doodle!

Term works in finance, but nobody's perfect.

Revie

She/They

Revie joined the furry fandom locally in 2018 but has been drawing furry and anthropomorphic artwork since the age of 10! She is well known as a small deer… or tiger… or fox, we lose track of all the characters!

Revie originally began supporting the Lincs Furs team back in 2022 and has recently returned after a break to complete her work probation. She can usually be seen at meets trying to use her best “teacher voice”, filming shameless TikToks or doodling quietly in a corner.

In her spare time, Revie enjoys drawing, writing, and playing video games. She also has a love for all things cute and cozy, and can often be found snuggling with her pets or indulging in some self-care.

WARNING: Do not mention D&D to Revie unless you are willing to engage in several hours of conversation.
