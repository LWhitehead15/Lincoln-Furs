Contact Us

The best ways to get in contact is by messaging an admin privately or speaking up in our online spaces, such as our Telegram group chat or Discord server.

The staff team may not respond immediately, but they hope not to take too long following up on queries or requests. Please don't contact admins about problems outside the meets or Lincoln Furs online spaces unless they are legally relevant. We are limited to safeguarding what we maintain.
